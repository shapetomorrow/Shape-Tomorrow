A Narrative First-Person Video Game About Today's Environmental Issues

Be The Mayor of a City

Face choices in your professional life that will have an impact on your city and the environment. You have 12 months to make your city an ecological and economic role model in order to obtain the organization of the Green Conference at the end of the year.

Fight For The Environment

Engaging

Shape Tomorrow will make you witness the impact of your decisions and make you aware of the ecological challenges that we face today.

Compelling

Follow a thrilling storyline with a wide array of characters, each with their own personalities and issues, in a stunning visual setting powered by Unreal Engine 4.
